Electromagnetics Global Summit 2023

The Electromagnetics Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the researchers affiliate network in a different country every four years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals.

The Electromagnetics Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and scientist who comprise the affiliate network of research in a different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the theory of electromagnetism, free culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, wikipedians, Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together
